Financial Advice

Every new project requires funds and every existing project requires additional funds. We offer you end to end services wherein we prepare all the necessary documents required for your loan proposal and provide all the assistance that is needed to get your loan sanctioned. We have a dedicated team having combined experience of over 25 years in this field and we specialize in the following areas:

Any entrepreneur, existing or new, needs to prepare the financial viability of the project he is planning to undertake. Preparation of this Financial Viability Report/Project Report is a highly technical work where a lot of parameters have to be taken care of. An expert hand is required to prepare a project report. We give you that expert hand.

Any business entity/ manufacturing concern looking to purchase new machinery and getting the same financed from bank/financial institution needs to prepare a Term Loan Proposal. Getting a term loan is beneficial for any concern as it helps it avail various subsidies offered by state and central governments.

Every entity that is enjoying loan facilities like Working Capital, Term Loan etc. need to submit to the banks/financial institutions their Estimations, Projections and CMA Data every year. We prepare for you all these requisite documents in a way your lender needs. We also offer assistance services that help you get sanction/renewal/enhancement of the loan facilities that have been applied for.

Many Entrepreneurs have ideas which are attractive for any lender but do not know the right path to proceed and present their proposal before a lender. Loan Syndication include, Working Capital, Term Loan, Loan against Property (LAP), Buyers Credit, Letter of Credit (LC), Bank Guarantee (BG), Packing Credit, Bill Discounting Limit, Lease Rent Discounting (LRD), Consortium Financing etc. We offer debt syndication services wherein we get your loans processed and help you avail credit facilities.

Banks include a lot of formalities in the terms of sanction; some have to be completed on a monthly basis, some on quarterly and half yearly basis. We provide assistance services where we prepare these documents as and when required.

We also provide Housing Loan, Education Loan, Personal Loan, and Commercial Loan Syndication Services. So, anyone looking to take such loans may contact us. We put in all efforts to provide our clients with the best possible deals.

Running a business always bears a risk and there are times when both external and internal forces cause a business model to fail or slowdown considerably. In these circumstances, loans and other credit facilities availed by these businesses become a burden. We understand the prospects of your business and prepare for you a restructuring proposal to be presented to your lender. We also provide assistance services in getting your restructuring proposal processed and also put in efforts to provide you with maximum possible relief.

One Time Settlement is the last resort for a business whose credit arrangements have become bad. We assist you in forming a proper one time settlement proposal to be offered to the bank/financial institution so that the client pays its dues without further default.

We advice you on the parameters which need to be taken care for getting good credit rating. Also we also help in correspondence with credit rating agencies.
